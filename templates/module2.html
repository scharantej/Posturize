Module 2: Reading Body Language Cues

In this module, we will discuss how to read and interpret body language cues. We will cover the following topics:

  • Facial Expressions: Facial expressions can reveal a person's emotions and intentions.
  • Eye Contact: Eye contact can be a sign of interest, attention, or dominance.
  • Body Posture: Body posture can indicate a person's mood, confidence, and openness.
  • Gestures: Gestures can be used to communicate thoughts, feelings, and intentions.
  • Proxemics: Proxemics refers to the use of personal space. It can indicate a person's level of comfort and intimacy with another person.
  • Haptics: Haptics refers to touch. It can be used to communicate affection, support, or dominance.

By understanding how to read and interpret body language cues, you can better understand the thoughts, feelings, and intentions of others. This can help you to communicate more effectively and build stronger relationships.
