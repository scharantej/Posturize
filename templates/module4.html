Module 4: Body Language Mistakes to Avoid

In this module, we will discuss some common body language mistakes that you should avoid. These mistakes can make you appear unprofessional, uninterested, or even dishonest.

  • Poor Eye Contact: Avoiding eye contact can make you appear shifty or untrustworthy.
  • Fidgeting: Fidgeting can be a sign of nervousness or anxiety.
  • Slouching: Slouching can make you appear lazy or disinterested.
  • Crossing Your Arms: Crossing your arms can be a sign of defensiveness or hostility.
  • Putting Your Hands in Your Pockets: Putting your hands in your pockets can make you appear bored or disinterested.
  • Touching Your Face: Touching your face can be a sign of insecurity or dishonesty.

By avoiding these common body language mistakes, you can make a better impression on others and communicate more effectively.
